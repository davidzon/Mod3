The Light We Carry

Michelle Obama

When they go low, you write another book. In an inspiring follow-up to her bestselling memoir Becoming, the former First Lady of the United States shares practical wisdom and powerful strategies for staying hopeful and balanced through difficult times.

Visual Thinking

Temple Grandin

Twenty-five years ago, author and educator Temple Grandin changed mainstream understanding of autism with her memoir, Thinking in Pictures. Her new book expands on the theme, celebrating visual/spacial cognition, intuitive design, and the critical importance of those who think differently. In fact, Grandin argues persuasively that today's problems require neurodiverse thinking.
